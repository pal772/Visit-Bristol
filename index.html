About Bristol

Bristol is a vibrant, diverse and innovative city. It's located just 120 miles west of London and is the largest city in the south west of England. The city is situated on the river Frome and river Avon.


Its former city centre port is now a culture hub, the Harbourside, where the M Shed museum explres local social and industrial heratiage. The harbours 19th centery warehouses now contain restaurants, shops and cultural institutions such as contemporary art gallery The Arnolfini.

Top 5 places to visit in Bristol

SS Great Britain

The SS Great Britian is a museum ship and former passanger steamship, that was advanced for her time. She was the largest passenger ship in the world from 1845 to 1854. she was designed by Isambard kingdom Brunel, for the great western steamship company's transatlantic service between Bristol and New York City.
